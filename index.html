AI-Powered Detection

On-device machine learning automatically finds your baby in videos and detects 13 distinct actions including crawling, walking, laughing, clapping, and more.

Themed Highlight Reels

Choose from five curated themes like Movement, Expression, Social, and Family Moments to tell your baby's story your way.

Full Customization

Add crossfade or fade-to-black transitions, choose background music, overlay captions with your child's name, and pick from beautiful font styles.

Private by Design

All AI processing runs entirely on your device. Your baby's videos never leave your phone. No cloud uploads, no internet required.

Fast Processing

Smart encoding creates your highlight reel in minutes, even with transitions, music, and captions applied.

Easy Sharing

Save to your Photos Library, share via Messages, or export to Files. Preview your reel before saving and see duration and file size at a glance.

How it works

1

Select and tag videos

Choose videos from your library and let TinyTakes detect your baby's actions. Review the tags and approve the clips you want to use.

2

Pick a date range and theme

Select a time period and a theme like Movement, Expression, or Family Moments. TinyTakes picks the best approved clips for you.

3

Customize your reel

Add transitions, background music, captions, and choose a font style that fits your family's personality.

4

Preview and share

Watch your highlight reel, then save it to your library or share it with family and friends.
