100% On-Device AI

Every model runs entirely on your iPhone. Your media never leave your phone, no data is sent to the cloud, and no internet connection is needed. Your family's privacy is never compromised.

You're in Control

TinyTakes detects 14 distinct actions like walking, crawling, playing, and eating. Review what the AI found and modify any label before it's used. You always have the final say.

Themed Highlight Reels

Choose from curated themes (Movement, Expression, Social) to tell your story.

Full Customization

Add crossfade or fade-to-black transitions, choose background music, overlay captions, and pick a font style.

Fast Processing

Smart encoding creates your highlight reel in minutes, with transitions, music, and captions applied.

Easy Sharing

Save to your Photos Library. Preview your reel before saving and see duration and file size at a glance.

How it works

1

Select and tag media

Choose media from your library and let TinyTakes detect your little one's actions. Review the tags and approve the clips you want to use.

2

Pick a date range and theme

Select a time period and theme. TinyTakes recommends the best approved clips for you.

3

Customize your reel

Add transitions, background music, captions, and choose a font style that fits your family's personality.

4

Preview and share

Watch your highlight reel, then save it to your library or share it with family and friends.
