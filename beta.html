Join the TinyTakes Beta

TinyTakes is currently in beta testing via Apple's TestFlight. Follow the steps below to get started.

1. Install TestFlight

TestFlight is Apple's official app for beta testing. Download it for free from the App Store.

Open TestFlight in the App Store →

2. Open the Beta Link

Tap the link below on your iPhone to open the TinyTakes beta invitation in TestFlight. You'll be asked to accept the invite.

Join TinyTakes Beta on TestFlight →

3. Install TinyTakes

Once you've accepted the invitation, tap Install to download TinyTakes. The app will appear on your home screen just like any other app.

4. Start Capturing Moments

Open TinyTakes, grant full access to your photo library, and start creating highlight reels of your little one's best moments. Updates will be delivered automatically through TestFlight.

Requirements

  • iPhone with iOS 15.1 or later
  • TestFlight app installed
  • An Apple ID

Need help?

If you run into any issues joining the beta or using the app, reach out to us at tinytakesapp@gmail.com or submit feedback through our website or directly in the app.
